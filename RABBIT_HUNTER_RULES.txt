OVERVIEW
--------
Rabbit Hunter is a side game played alongside a standard round
of golf. One "Rabbit" is in play at a time. Players compete to
capture and hold the Rabbit — and defend it — hole by hole.
A player earns a payout based on how many holes they hold the
Rabbit on the Front 9 and Back 9.

------------------------------------------------------------

BUY-IN & POT
------------
- Each player contributes a BUY-IN amount (e.g. $10/player).
- Total Pot = BUY-IN × Number of Players
  Example: 3 players × $10 = $30 total pot

Payout Split:
  - Front 9 Winner  →  40% of total pot
  - Back 9 Winner   →  60% of total pot

The player who holds the Rabbit on the most holes within
each 9-hole segment wins that segment's share of the pot.

------------------------------------------------------------

HOLE RULES (applied in priority order each hole)
-------------------------------------------------

RULE 1 — SUPER CAPTURE (Highest Priority)
  A player who makes a NATURAL BIRDIE (gross score < par,
  no handicap strokes used) immediately captures the Rabbit,
  regardless of any other outcome on that hole.
  This overrides Rule 2 and Rule 3.

RULE 2 — STANDARD CAPTURE / KNOCK LOOSE
  The player with the LONE LOW NET score on a hole (no ties)
  either:
    a) CAPTURES the Rabbit — if the Rabbit is currently FREE.
    b) KNOCKS IT LOOSE — if another player currently holds it.
       The Rabbit becomes FREE; it is NOT transferred directly
       to the knock-loose player. It is up for grabs next hole.

RULE 3 — DOUBLE BOGEY PENALTY
  If the current Rabbit HOLDER posts a gross score of
  PAR + 2 or worse (double bogey or worse), the Rabbit is
  automatically released — regardless of how other players
  scored. The Rabbit becomes FREE for the next hole.

RULE 4 — TIE DEFENSE
  If 2 or more players tie for the low net score on a hole,
  the Rabbit does NOT change hands:
    - If a player currently holds the Rabbit → they keep it.
    - If the Rabbit is free → it remains free.

------------------------------------------------------------

STATUS DEFINITIONS
------------------
  FREE     — No one currently holds the Rabbit. Any player
              can capture it on the next hole.
  HELD     — A specific player currently holds the Rabbit.
              They earn credit for each hole they hold it.
  RELEASED — The holder triggered a Double Bogey Penalty;
              the Rabbit is now FREE starting next hole.

------------------------------------------------------------

SCORING & WINNER DETERMINATION
-------------------------------
- Each hole a player HOLDS the Rabbit counts as +1 for them
  in that 9-hole segment.
- The player with the MOST holes held in the Front 9 wins
  40% of the pot.
- The player with the MOST holes held in the Back 9 wins
  60% of the pot.
- In the event of a tie in holes held within a segment, the
  tied players split that segment's payout equally.

------------------------------------------------------------

QUICK REFERENCE SUMMARY
------------------------
  Lone Low Net (Rabbit FREE)    →  Capture Rabbit
  Lone Low Net (Rabbit HELD)    →  Knock Loose → Rabbit becomes FREE
  Natural Birdie (Gross < Par)  →  Super Capture (overrides all)
  Double Bogey (Gross ≥ Par+2)  →  Holder releases Rabbit (FREE)
  Tie for Low Net               →  No change (holder defends)

  Front 9 Payout  =  40% of pot
  Back 9 Payout   =  60% of pot
  Total Pot       =  BUY-IN × Players
