COD GOLF APP - TESTER INSTRUCTIONS
==================================

Here is how to set up and use the COD Golf App for our next round!

1. INSTALLAPP (Add to Home Screen)
----------------------------------
This runs as a full-screen app on your phone.

* iPhone (Safari):
  1. Open the link in Safari.
  2. Tap the "Share" icon (square with arrow up) at the bottom.
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  4. Tap "Add". The icon will appear on your phone like a real app.

* Android (Chrome):
  1. Open the link in Chrome.
  2. Tap the menu (three dots) in the top right.
  3. Tap "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen".
  4. Confirm to install.

2. STARTING A ROUND
-------------------
1. Open the App.
2. Tap "MANAGE" under history to add player names to the Roster if needed.
3. Select 4 Players from the dropdowns.
4. Choose "C.O.D. (6-6-6)" or "2-Man Scramble".
5. Set the Pot Amount (usually $20).
6. Click "START ROUND".

3. SCORING (Important!)
-----------------------
* ENTERING PAR: Tap the BIG PAR NUMBER in the center of the score button to instantly set that player closer to Par.
* ADJUSTING: Use the (+) and (-) buttons to adjust the score if needed.
* JUNK BETS: If playing Junk (Greenies/Sandies), you can toggle the G/S/P buttons under each player's score.
* NAVIGATION: The app auto-advances after everyone has a score. You can also swipe or use arrows at the top.

4. END OF ROUND
---------------
1. After Hole 18, you will see the Round Summary card.
2. Scroll down to see Match Results, Junk Payouts, and Net Scores.
3. Tap "SHARE IMAGE SUMMARY" to generate a picture of the results and scorecard to text to the group!

----------------------------------
Have fun testing! Let me know if you find any bugs.
