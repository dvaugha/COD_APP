A 4-Player Rotational Partner Game

‚õ≥ THE FORMAT

The 18-hole round is split into three 6-hole "Segments". Each segment is a separate match with a new partner.

  • HOLES 1-6 (CARTS): Partners are determined by cart pairings.
  • HOLES 7-12 (OPPOSITES): Partners swap (Driver of Cart 1 pairs with Passenger of Cart 2).
  • HOLES 13-18 (DRIVERS): Partners swap again (Drivers vs Passengers).

SCORING

Each segment is played as Net Best Ball Match Play.

  • Each team takes their single best Net Score on the hole.
  • Lower score wins the hole (1 Point).
  • Ties result in no points (Push).
  • The team with the most points at the end of the 6-hole segment wins the bet.
  • If points are tied at the end (e.g., 3-3), the segment is a WASH (No money exchanges hands).

THE PRESS

If a team is losing, they may have the option to PRESS. This creates a high-stakes scenario on the final hole of the segment (Holes 6, 12, and 18).

  • PRESS SAVE (Type 2): If the pressing team WINS the last hole, the entire segment bet is SAVED (Washed/Tied).
  • PRESS WIN (Type 1): If the pressing team WINS the last hole, they WIN the segment bet outright.
  • PRESS LOSS: If the pressing team LOSES the last hole, they lose DOUBLE the original bet.
  • If the last hole is tied, the press is ignored and the segment is decided by total points as usual.

THE JUNK DRAWER

Optional side bets that pay out individually. These are played as a Group Pot.

Example: Everyone puts in $1. Pot is $4. If you are the only winner, you get $4 (Net +$3). If two people win, they split ($2 each).
